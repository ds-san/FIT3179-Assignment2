Suicide Around the World

Every year, hundreds of thousands of people commit suicide around the world. This is not specific to specific regions but is more prevalent in some than others. The visualisations on this page aim to provide an insight of how prevalent an suicide suicide has been for decades

Suicides commited around the globe

Shown below is a map of the world showing data of suicides per 100,000 people in each country. Not enough data from all countries was found and those are represented by the grey colour. Hovering the mouse on top of the locations show how many people have commited suicide in which region around the world.

Further Analysis

Lets see how people commiting suicide has changed over the years.

It's necessary to further understand information on this phenomenon as knowing it is happening isn't enough. Among these people commiting suicide we can look into different factors such as their age groups and gender to try to determine any patterns that may emerge.

In the following graphs click on the specific category in their own legend to highlight them in their visualisations or use the sliders provided under them to change what year they show information from
